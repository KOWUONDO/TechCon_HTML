History of TechCon

TechCon started in 2010 as a small developer meet-up and has grown into one of the most influential technology conferences in Africa. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of developers, innovators, and entrepreneurs from across the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the next generation of tech leaders by creating an open platform for learning, collaboration, and innovation. We believe that technology can change lives and communities when placed in the right hands.

Notable Past Speakers

Grace Mwangi

Grace is a cybersecurity expert and founder of SafeWeb Africa. She spoke on the future of digital privacy and cyber laws in Africa.

James Kimani

James is a machine learning engineer at Intel. His TechCon session on AI for small businesses received a standing ovation.

Aisha Mohammed

Aisha is a software developer and community leader who promotes women in tech. Her talk inspired over 500 young female developers in 2023.
